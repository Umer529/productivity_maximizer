TROUBLESHOOTING
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Backend won't start?
A: Check Node.js 18+, run npm install, port 5000 free

Q: Frontend can't connect?
A: Update BASE_URL in src/lib/apiClient.ts to your IP

Q: ML predictions timeout?
A: Ensure Python 3.8+, artifacts/ has model files

Q: No tasks in schedule?
A: Check deadline set, status != 'completed'

Q: Analytics showing zeros?
A: Complete at least one focus session first
